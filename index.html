Who we Are

As far as I can remember

"I always wanted to be a gangster." That didn't happen. I loved school too much!

Thus, instead of hustlin', I chose a PC and "Grand Theft Auto" to blow off steam in between sessions of teaching myself the arts of graphic design. This started 16 years ago.

Personal Interests

When not designing or coding a layout I fill my time with either of the following:

  • I love to travel;
  • I read a lot of SciFi literature;
  • I am a hip-hop beatmaker;
  • And an avid snowboarder in winter.
